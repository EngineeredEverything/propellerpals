Frequently Asked Questions

What is Propeller Pals?

Propeller Pals is an educational STEM media brand for children ages 4-8. Set in Propeller Town (inspired by Charleston, SC), it features characters like Connie the Containership, Heli the Helicopter, and Drifty the Drone who teach kids about propulsion, navigation, weather, logistics, and teamwork through fun adventures.

What products do you sell?

We offer STEM-focused story books ($12.99), plush character toys ($19.99), and activity kits ($14.99). All products are designed to be educational, safe, and fun for children ages 4-8.

How do I order?

You can order directly from our Shop section above. We accept all major credit cards and ship throughout the United States. Orders typically ship within 2-3 business days.

What age is Propeller Pals for?

Propeller Pals is designed for children ages 4-8. The content is engaging enough for the whole family while being age-appropriate and educational for young learners.
